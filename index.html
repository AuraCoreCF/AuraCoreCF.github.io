Coherence-Field Cognitive Architecture
AuraCoreCF is a research project exploring coherence-based cognitive architectures, emergent identity systems, and the relationship between computational structure and physical coherence models.

The project investigates how layered cognitive fields, coherence stabilization, and structural self-consistency may produce persistent reasoning systems that extend beyond traditional stateless AI architectures.
