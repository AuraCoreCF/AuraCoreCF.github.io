Architecture Overview

A field-oriented model for persistent cognition and identity behavior.

AuraCoreCF describes a coherence-centered cognitive architecture intended to support continuity, internal constraint handling, and more stable identity expression than standard stateless interaction loops.

Primary design intent

The architecture treats coherence, continuity, and self-consistency as governing conditions. Instead of focusing only on prompt-response behavior, it frames cognition as a managed field of interacting structures.

Key architectural themes

  • Persistent reasoning across sessions or state transitions
  • Identity stabilization rather than accidental persona drift
  • Constraint-aware behavior under changing context
  • Visible governance boundaries and failure-mode handling
