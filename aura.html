Architecture Overview

A field-oriented architecture for persistent cognition.

AuraCoreCF describes a coherence-centered cognitive architecture intended to support continuity, internal constraint handling, self-consistency, and more stable identity behavior than conventional stateless loops.

Primary design intent

The architecture treats coherence, continuity, and structural self-maintenance as governing conditions. It is organized more like a managed field of interacting subsystems than a simple one-shot text engine.

Key themes

  • Persistent reasoning across sessions or state transitions
  • Identity stabilization instead of accidental persona drift
  • Constraint-aware behavior under changing context
  • Visible governance boundaries and failure-mode handling
