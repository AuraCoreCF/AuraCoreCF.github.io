Physics Overview

Coherence, decoherence, and structural interpretation.

This section is the public landing point for the physics side of AuraCoreCF. It is designed to summarize the conceptual framing, highlight technical directions, and connect readers to formal papers and research notes as they are published.

Research orientation

The physics work associated with AuraCoreCF examines whether coherence can be treated as a meaningful structural principle, rather than only as an observational descriptor. The goal is not to overstate claims, but to clarify the conceptual bridge between coherence-driven interpretations and the design of stable cognitive systems.

Current focus

  • Apparatus-dependent decoherence framing
  • Coherence as a constraint on system-level organization
  • Interpretive links between physical coherence and computational consistency
  • Clear separation between empirical claims, theoretical extensions, and speculative synthesis
